HTTP协议报文简介¶

CRLF: \r\n

METHOD: HTTP请求，GET、POST、PUT、DELETE…

URI: 统一资源标识符，比如/，/index.html…

HTTPVersion: HTTP协议的版本号，比如HTTP/1.1，HTTP/2

METHOD<SPACE>URI<SPACE>HTTPVersion<CRLF>
